Who Are We


Abhishek, Arjun CH, Arjun U and Ayush, the dynamic Squad behind Group 8, have collaborated to develop this exceptional e-commerce website dedicated to clothing. With their combined expertise and passion for technology, they have crafted this platform that seamlessly connects fashion enthusiasts with their favorite apparel. Together, the Team have synergized their skills and knowledge to build UrbanTrove a compelling e-commerce platform that showcases the latest fashion trends, offers seamless navigation, and ensures a delightful shopping experience. With their dedication and expertise, they have crafted UrbanTrove that goes beyond just selling clothes; it represents a gateway to express one's style and individuality.


Whether you're a fashion enthusiast seeking the latest trends or a fashion brand looking to expand your reach, UrbanTrove is the perfect destination. Get ready to embark on a fashionable journey with their meticulously crafted platform.
